U.G IT
The curriculum will enable students with conceptual knowledge and technical skills to forge ahead in the field of IT, while inculcating deep moral and ethical values to achieve excellence, by providing a vibrant academic and research environment in collaboration with industry.

U.G AI & DS
The curriculum will enable students to utilize their proficiencies in the fundamental knowledge of basic sciences, mathematics, Artificial Intelligence, data science and statistics to build systems that require management and analysis of large volumes of data.

M.Tech IT
The curriculum will enable students to excel professionally by adapting to the dynamic needs of the industry, academia and research in the field of Information Technology and contribute to advancement of information technology by means of research and lifelong learning.
